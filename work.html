Icons.

My concept was to illustrate icons for a tourist tour through Bo-Kaap, which is world wide famous for its tiny, colourful houses.

The first icon represents the houses, the second one the islamic culture, and the third, represents the famous Noon-Gun which fires every day at noon. The fourth icon illustrates the small museum in Bo-Kaap. Furthermore, the fifth icon illustrates the well known spice shop situated in Wale Street and the last icon represents the vast variety of Halal foods and the restaurants in Bo-Kaap.

All six icons are in a colourful style to correlate with the atmosphere and imagery of Bo-Kaap.

Magazine.

My magazine concept deals with stories and reports from unusual and extreme settings. Hence, the name, “No Limits“.

Featured in this issue is the British extreme surviver, Ed Stafford. The first person to walk the entire length of the Amazon River.

The magazine targets a male audience of every age, but is not biased against a female audience either.

The magazine is separated into several different chapters; culinary, adventure, lifestyle, future, adrenalin, nature and records. The layout comprises of bold, strong colours and sharp patterns to convey a feeling of masculinity and fearlessness.

Video

Logan.

The task was to create a title sequence for a movie or series in Adobe After Effects.

I chose to make a title sequence for the movie, Logan. My idea was to introduce all the main characters of the movie and give each individual a specific effect and sound to describe the personlity of each character. Over all, I kept the characters in a comic and drawn style.

The title sequence starts with the injured and heavy breathing Logan, followed by Charles Xavier. I made the light fade away and heart beat out of sync to convay the thought that Chales is getting older and weaker. Following Charles, I placed Caliban, his effect is that he fades away while being outside because he is not able to be in the sunlight since it makes him weaker and injures him.

The next character introduced is the right hand of the evil Dr Rice, Donald Pierce. He appears with dramatic smoke in the background and he loads his rifel to illustrate that he is one of the bad guys. Following, appears his boss, Dr Zander Rice. The viewer can see a phone, in the background which plays a video his crew doing experiments on a young girl.

The second last character introduced is, Gabriela, who has a small part in the movie but still an important role as she connects Laura with Logan. Last but not least, I show Laura Kinney. I purposely placed her at the end and Logan in the beginning because she is overtaking the role of Wolverine and is becomng more powerful than expected.

Website

Chicken Licken.

The brief for this page was to redesign an existing brand which had a poorly designed website. However, the redesigned website should complement the original brand design. I decided to remake the website of Chicken Licken.

The website is static. I recreated the following pages within the website; the homepage, two menu pages, the ordering page, the contact page and a video gallery page.

The home page displays the latest specials and the latest famous TV adverts for which Chicken Licken is known. I tried to organise the menu page since the form of the original was very confusing as it was juat an uploaded PDF. I seperated the menu into different sections, where the user would be able to click to get the further information and prices.

The ordering button will redirect the user to an ordering form where the ordering and delivery options will be displayed.

The contact page displays the contact details and social media links of Chicken Linken. It further displays the navigation routes to the various Chicken Linken stores via Google Maps.

Last, but not least I added a video gallery page which shows more of the famouse and amusing Chicken Licken TV comercials along with a few interviews.

Dashoard.

In this project I learnt how to use the vue.js plugin to make different HTML templates. I also learnt how to use SCSS and how to compile the different SCSS files to one CSS file.

The brief was to create a dashboard with different themes. I created a login page, a dashboard page and messaging page for a restaurant, where the user can view specific statistics of the user habits at the restaurant.

The dashboard displays the statistics of what and when the user is consuming at the restaurant. It further displays the user´s favourite things at the restaurant. The message page shows a message application which codes I received from an open source page. I only modified the codes to my needs.

The different modes which I made were split in different SCSS files. These modes are two colour modes and a rasta mode, which was added just for fun.
