THAT´S ME

Felix Talla

Hi, I am Felix!

I am a native German and moved to South Africa two years ago. After I completed my highschool I went to Zambia to do a volunteer year.

In Cape Town, I started my offical design career as a student at Friends of Design, where I completed two different courses, the Print and Publishing course and the Web Publishing and Interactive Media course. As a result, I attained a lot of skills and can now offer knowledge on good design and a diverse array of programs.

I have knowledge in the Adobe programs, Photoshop, Illustrator, inDesign, After Effects and Premier Pro. Furthermore, I know the basics of camera use, shooting and sound editing.

I have developed a passion for the field of web design and development. I know how to use HTML, CSS and the fundamentals of JavaScript and Jquery, as well as the basics of PHP.

My passion has always been in design and art. I hope to start this career successfully and I am always strieving to attain more knowledge to broaden my skill set.

I take pride in my work and always aim to produce work of high quality. I believe that my work is a direct reflection of my work ethic.
